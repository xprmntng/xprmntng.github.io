Committed to Our Members

Fredericksburg Country Club was founded in 1925 as the premier location in Fredericksburg to enjoy excellent food, catch up with friends, take a swim, or practice your backhand. Our members make Fredericksburg Country Club so special — they are a compilation of the finest residents in Fredericksburg, many with long family histories at our Country Club. The majestic clubhouse at the Fredericksburg Country Club, situated just south of the city of Fredericksburg, is nestled close to the Rappahannock River on Tidewater Trail. The Club is considered the area's best family-oriented club and is truly a "home away from home" providing a safe and secure recreational environment for members and guests.

Fredericksburg Country Club’s mission is to be a socially-oriented family country club, providing the highest standard of service, products, golf and facilities.
